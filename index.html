Zhan (Michael) Shi, Ph.D.

Associate Professor of Information Systems
W.P. Carey School of Business, Arizona State University

I work at the interface of economics and computation, with applications in platform-based markets, social media, and innovation. I am excited about the ongoing transformation of economics driven by the data and computation revolution.

Before coming to Phoenix, I worked on my Ph.D. in economics at The University of Texas at Austin. I also studied economics and mathematics at Peking University, Beijing, China.
